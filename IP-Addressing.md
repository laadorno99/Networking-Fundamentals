# IP Addressing Basics

Based on my introductory studies in networking and security.

## IPv4 Addresses
* Every device on a network needs a unique IP address to communicate.
* IPv4 is the most common version, written as four numbers (e.g., 192.168.1.1).

## Public vs. Private IPs
* **Public IP:** Used to communicate over the internet.
* **Private IP:** Used inside a local network (like your home or school).

## Why it matters for Security
* Knowing the IP address of a device helps in identifying the source of a threat or a connection in a firewall log.
